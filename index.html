ROAS is a signal. Profit is the decision.

A high ROAS can hide unprofitable ads when margins, returns, and fees are left out. AdProfit aligns Meta spend with real store outcomes so you optimize for money in the bank—not dashboard vanity.

  • Compare ad spend to net revenue and estimated profit, not just attributed sales.
  • Catch campaigns that look efficient on paper but drain budget after costs.
  • Make faster, calmer decisions with one source of truth for growth and finance.

Stop guessing which ads to scale. Know which ones actually pay for themselves.
