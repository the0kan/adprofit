ROAS alone is not enough

Return on ad spend ignores margin, fees, and whether purchases are incremental. Teams scale the wrong campaigns and finance sees the mismatch weeks later.

  • Surface metrics

    Channel dashboards celebrate spend efficiency without tying it to money retained after costs.

  • Siloed data

    Ads live in Meta while truth lives in spreadsheets — slow, fragile, and impossible at scale.

  • Slow decisions

    Without a single profit-oriented view, pauses and budget shifts arrive too late to protect margin.

FAQ

Straight answers for evaluation and rollout.

Where are Meta tokens stored?
Only on the AdProfit API. They are encrypted in Neon PostgreSQL and never returned to the browser.
Do I need the command line?
No. Connect Meta, pick an account, and load campaigns entirely through the dashboard UI.
Is profit exact?
The product clearly labels estimated profit using a fixed margin on purchase value until deeper COGS modeling ships.
When does billing go live?
Stripe checkout is coming soon. Architecture is ready; activation is a configuration step.
