Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

AdProfit is an analytics platform for e-commerce brands. It helps you connect advertising and store data—such as Meta Ads, WooCommerce, and Shopify—to understand spend, revenue, profitability, and related metrics in one workspace.

This policy describes how we may collect, use, and protect information when you use AdProfit. It is intended for an early-stage product; we will update it as the service evolves.

Information we may process

Depending on how you use the product, we may process categories such as:

  • Account information. Such as your name, email address, and credentials you use to access AdProfit.
  • Meta Ads connection data. When you connect Meta, we may process identifiers, campaign and performance metrics, and other data made available through Meta’s APIs, subject to your permissions and Meta’s terms.
  • WooCommerce and Shopify data. When you connect a store, we may process order, product, customer, or revenue-related data as exposed by those platforms’ integrations, to power reporting and analysis.
  • Analytics and usage data. Such as how you interact with the product, diagnostic logs, and technical information needed to operate and improve the service.

How we use information

We use this information to:

  • Provide dashboard reporting and profitability analysis;
  • Operate and maintain integrations with third-party platforms you connect;
  • Improve the product, fix issues, and understand aggregate usage patterns;
  • Communicate with you about the service where appropriate.

Sharing and subprocessors

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers and infrastructure partners who help us host, secure, or operate AdProfit, and with integrated platforms (such as Meta, WooCommerce, or Shopify) as needed to provide features you enable. Those parties process data under their own terms and our instructions where applicable.

Your choices and contact

You may contact us to ask about access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to legal and operational requirements. For privacy-related requests, email privacy@adprofit.app.
