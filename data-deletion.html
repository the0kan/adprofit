Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 2026

Request deletion of your data

If you use AdProfit and want your data deleted, you can submit a request by contacting our privacy team.

Depending on your workspace and integrations, deletion may include account data, connected integration data (such as Meta Ads, WooCommerce, and Shopify connection-related data), and related analytics records used in AdProfit reporting.

How to request deletion

Email your request to privacy@adprofit.app from the account email associated with your workspace, and include enough detail for us to identify your account.

We review and process valid requests within a reasonable time, subject to legal, security, and operational requirements.
